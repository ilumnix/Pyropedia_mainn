Special Fire Force Company 1

Leonard Burns

Captain

Leonard Burns

Leonard Burns, the Battalion Commander of Special Fire Force Company 1, is first portrayed as a severe character with ties to the covert Evangelist organization. However, as the story unfolds, he develops into a multifaceted figure who confronts his past and seeks redemption. He saves Shinra from her Infernal Mother, loses faith in Holy Sol Temple after uncovering Evangelist's involvement, sends Joker to investigate Haijima Industries, arrests Obi, defeats Shinra with Joker's help, and attacks Infernal from the shadows. His journey entails sacrifice and leadership development, and it ultimately changes the outcome of the story, adding complexity to it.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Leonard's Voltage Nova Ignition Ability allows him to improve his physical powers by igniting flames within his body, increasing his firepower over time. He also Master's Hand to Hand Combat with Immense Durability (3 stages), and Immense Strength (4 stages).

Karim Flam

Lieutenant

Karim Flam

Karim Flam, with his distinct appearance of shaved sides, small, lazy dark eyes, and straight, dark gray hair, transforms from a mysterious and reserved individual to a respected leader in "Fire Force." He wears a light overcoat-like garment with a high collar and dark cassock and carries a cross-like pendant instead of bunker gear. Karim's growth is characterized by loyalty, determination, and intellectualism, as he uses musical instruments and headphones in warfare. His path of atonement and self-discovery, motivated by previous pain, leads to deep friendships and spiritual progress, enhancing the series with themes of camaraderie and personal development.

Ability: Second Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Karim, a Second Generation, can make ice by "Thermoacoustic Refrigeration," which converts thermal energy into sound and lowers the temperature. This ability allows him to unleash assaults and incapacitate Infernals. Karim's skill is analogous to air conditioning, and he may utilize his musical instrument to amp up the effect. He also carries a bell, which makes startling noises while reducing heat and collecting it for his instrument. His exquisite control enables him to create air holes and suddenly freeze entire Firestorms. His smart and quick thinking abilities make him a deadly opponent.

Huo Yan Li

Former Captain

Huo Yan Li

Huo Yan Li, known for his short, shaggy blond hair and dark clothes ornamented with a cross-like pendant, begins his voyage in "Fire Force" as a disciplined member of Company 4. His traumatic background, which includes the loss of his family to a fire, haunts him. He does, however, find encouragement from his ties with teammates such as Sister Iris and Captain Hague. Confronted with personal and external difficulties, he begins on a journey of self-discovery and repentance, eventually becoming a more compassionate and self-aware member of the Fire Force, despite losing an arm in an incident with Rekka's superiors.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Huo Yan, a Third Generation pyrokinetic, uses flames in combat and defeats Infernals with quick, accurate hand-to-hand actions despite losing his right arm.

Onyango

Former Lieutenant

Onyango

Onyango, an elderly bald guy with metal connections on his temples, wears a dark cassock and a light overcoat to match his unit's lieutenants. Despite his retirement and physical constraints, he stays committed to his duty. Throughout the series, Onyango grows profoundly as he faces his team's commitment, doubts the organization's motivations, and seeks truth in the midst of chaos. His character exemplifies power, dedication, and emotional depth, depicting a path of self-discovery while being a devoted and formidable team member.
