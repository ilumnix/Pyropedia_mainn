Onyango

Onyango, an elderly bald guy with metal connections on his temples, wears a dark cassock and a light overcoat to match his unit's lieutenants. Despite his retirement and physical constraints, he stays committed to his duty. Throughout the series, Onyango grows profoundly as he faces his team's commitment, doubts the organization's motivations, and seeks truth in the midst of chaos. His character exemplifies power, dedication, and emotional depth, depicting a path of self-discovery while being a devoted and formidable team member.
