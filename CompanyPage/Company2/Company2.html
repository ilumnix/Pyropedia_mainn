Special Fire Force Company 2

Gustav Honda

Captain

Gustav Honda

Gustav Honda, with his chiseled jawline and distinctive black-ringed pupils, wears the traditional bunker gear of the Special Fire Brigade, complete with cross-like symbols and patches showing his membership with the 2nd Brigade. Gustav, a skeptical scientist who relies on empirical facts, gains a new perspective after working with Company 7. He grows personally by developing close friendships and experiencing the human side of Infernals, questioning his ideas and blending scientific knowledge with spiritual understanding, ultimately becoming a more empathic and open-minded individual.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Gustav, a Third Generation, generates flames from his scalp and uses them for tremendous headbutts to repel items and slay Giant Infernals. He possesses incredible physical strength, speed, and durability, and can launch himself as a lethal missile.

Takeru Noto

Fire Officer

Takeru Noto

Takeru Noto, a tall young man with brunette hair and squinty eyes, appears to be a bold warrior in Company 2 of "Fire Force." His military uniform, layered for evasion, conceals metal prosthetics that replace his missing forearm and leg in combat. For formal events, he wears a dark cape and a headgear ornamented with a cross-shaped insignia. Throughout the series, he matures via his friendship with Shinra, talent improvement, overcoming adversity, and embracing collaboration, eventually becoming a valued part of his squad.

Ability: Second Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Takeru, a Third Generation Pyrokinetic, creates exploding flames in the shape of projectiles. Despite his enormous destructive potential, his nervousness and lack of control limit his powers. His dread of fire causes him to attack in a frenzy and with poor accuracy.
