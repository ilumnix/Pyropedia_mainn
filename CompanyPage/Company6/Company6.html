kayako Huang

Captain

Kayako Huang

Kayoko Huang, a tall woman with short black hair and light eyes, starts out as a talented technical specialist in "Fire Force." But she grows into a confident and capable Second Generation pyrokinetic. Her journey entails understanding the value of cooperation, personal development, and building meaningful relationships with her teammates. She wears a Special Fire Force jacket, a black dress, and high heels, highlighting her unique style. In battle, she wears a black surgical mask and dark green outfit embroidered with Holy Sol crosses. At the 6th Company Barracks, she chooses a white lab coat over a black shirt. Her surgical outfit during operations consists of a gown, gloves, and mask, with a visible scar on her left palm from an encounter with Adolla.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Kayoko, a Third Generation, utilizes her Ignition Ability in the form of an asklepian to expedite cell division, hence improving body regeneration. This enables her to treat diseases, mend wounds, and alleviate numerous symptoms. She can use the flames of other Third Generations to amplify regeneration and relieve pain immediately.

Asako Hague

Lieutenant

Asako Hague

Asako Hague, a short girl with pink hair and a considerable bust, resembles her grandfather, Sōichirō Hague, especially with her round glasses. Her outfit consists of a white collared shirt under a darker covering, as well as a white apron tied with a slash. She wears an armband with the Cross of the Holy Sol Temple, which represents Company 6. Asako, a member of Special Fire Force Company 8 , appears to be shy and easily scared. Throughout the series, she develops significantly as a character, becoming more confident and courageous as she faces various hardships alongside her friends. This development focuses on overcoming fear and gaining inner strength in the face of adversity.
