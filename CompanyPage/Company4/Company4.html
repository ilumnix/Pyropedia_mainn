Special Fire Force Company 1

Sōichirō Hague

Captain

Sōichirō Hague

Hague Sōichirō, a member of Special Fire Force Company 4, has major character development during "Fire Force." Initially portrayed as a faithful and serious fireman, Hague matures into a more open-minded individual who questions authority and explores his individuality. Despite his unassuming appearance, he has a toned, strong build from years of duty. His journey entails overcoming stereotypes, finding redemption, and eventually emerging as a successful leader within his team, all while developing relationships and facing his history.

Ability: None (Non-Powered Human)

Despite his lack of pyrokinetic talents, Hague was a skilled warrior who knew how to use firefighting tools successfully in warfare. He could quickly restrain targets with safety lines and use fire axes with devastating precision. Despite his age, he displayed quickness and durability, easily dealing with powerful opponents and withstanding strikes from Shinra and Gold until his limitations were reached.

Pan Ko Paat

Former Captain

Pan Ko Paat

Pan, a calm firefighter from Company 4, puts safety above all else. He is willing to work with perceived threats to reduce casualties, and he discreetly commemorates the fallen while maintaining a public posture.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Pan, a Third Generation, increases heat resistance using his talent, Buff Stacking, which is activated by his whistle. He boosts the abilities of other pyrokinetics, earning him the title 'Master Firefighter of Buffs' and a crucial role in Company 4's plans.

Ogun Montgomery

Second Class

Ogun Montgomery

Ogun Montgomery, with his dark skin, dreadlocks on shaved sides, and orange eyes with brilliant swirls, wears the Special Fire Force outfit with rolled-up sleeves and matching wristbands. In missions, he wears bunker gear, exposing his forearms. Throughout "Fire Force," Ogun develops from a brazen, competitive pyrokinetic to a mature hero. His relationships with Company 4, particularly with Shinra, foster personal development as he analyzes reasons and faces challenges. Ogun's commitment to justice is strengthened by acts of redemption and sacrifice, and he becomes an important partner in the fight against infernal threats.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Ogun, a Third Generation Pyrokinetic, is Company 4's Star Rookie, renowned for designing and welding flame weapons. His skill allows him to create spear and sword-like projectiles with powerful offensive and long-range capabilities. He also employs flames as boosters for a hoverboard, which allows him to move quickly across different terrains and in the air.
