Special Fire Force Company 3

Dr. Giovanni

Captain

Dr. Giovanni

Dr. Giovanni, who resembles a disease doctor, begins as a cold antagonist in "Fire Force," sending Evangelist agents to infiltrate Company 3. His look changes from black attire and a white cane to a skinnier Special Fire Force jacket with mechanics concealed beneath a big cloak. His past as a disillusioned scientist gradually develops, giving his character dimension. Interactions with allies and foes reveal his personal problems. Giovanni eventually goes through a redemption arc, questioning his commitment to the White-Clad and trying for redemption, demonstrating the series' deep character development.

Ability: Second Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Giovanni, a Second Generation pyrokinetic, uses external flames to operate secret devices and systems beneath his coat. He uses heated mechanical hands as projectiles, slashing objects with wires.
