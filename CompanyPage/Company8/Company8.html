Akitaru Obi

Captain

Akitaru Obi

Akitaru Obi, a well-built young man with short, slightly messy dark hair, shaved sides, and small dark brown eyes, wears bunker gear on the battlefield. His outfit consists of a black tank top, slacks, and a protective jacket with blue-lined decorations, which is accompanied by a jumper, neck protector, gloves, and black boots. Obi wields a variety of weapons and wears "8" patches to indicate his allegiance with the 8th Special Fire Brigade. Off duty, he wears a grey jumpsuit. Throughout "Fire Force," Obi's path takes him from a determined leader to a resilient captain, managing personal problems while instilling trust, teamwork, and a strong sense of justice in his unit.

Ability: Non-Powered Human

Akitaru's firefighting commendations were removed because he prioritized residents' lives over commands. His knowledge allows him to appraise circumstances and reassure allies. Despite lacking ignition skills, he maintains a healthy physique and can carry up to 30kg of equipment on missions. Captain Giovanni admires his incredible power, as evidenced by his efficient use of many weapons in battle. Akitaru strikes without hesitation, risking his life to generate opportunities or carry out plans. He devises plans on the spot, employing unique techniques such as train tracks as ladders and fire extinguisher foam as smokescreens. He's willing to take heavy damage to close up on formidable adversaries, even allowing himself to be struck by Benimaru explosions.

Takesa Hinawa

Lieutenant

Takehisa Hinawa

Takehisa Hinawa is a young man with short brown hair, bangs covering his brown eyes, and glasses. He wears bunker gear, featuring a dark tank top, trousers, and a protective jacket with cross-shaped buttons and blue-lined accents, along with a neck protector, gloves, and dark boots. On the battlefield, he carries various artillery. Off duty, he opts for an orange jumpsuit, dark shoes, and a headgear with changing designs. His attire bears "8" patches, signifying his affiliation with the 8th Special Fire Brigade. Initially a stoic and dutiful lieutenant, Hinawa gradually trusts Shinra, reveals his enigmatic past, and undergoes emotional growth, fostering greater openness with his comrades. His conflicts challenge his loyalties and convictions, culminating in significant character development characterized by a blend of duty and emotional depth.

Ability: Second Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Hinawa, a Second Generation, fights fires with weaponry such as submachine guns or handguns. His ability, "Bullet Speed Control," allows him to control bullet impact power by regulating propellant ignition. With "Trajectory Control," he adjusts bullet trajectory by shooting from different angles and distances, leaving a trail of fire. "Ricochet Control" allows him to rebound bullets off surfaces, increasing velocity. He also employs Special Fire Extinguishing Bullets to defeat and slow down Infernals.

Maki Oze

First Class

Maki Oze

Maki Oze, a thin and voluptuous young woman with wide violet eyes and long dark hair, is easily identifiable in her bunker suit emblazoned with "8" patches, denoting her participation in Special Fire Force Company 8. She wears a sleeveless top and black trousers, as well as a protective jacket and trousers with distinguishing blue lines. A neck protector, gloves, boots, and helmet round out her costume. For formal events, she wears a black jacket and skirt with a white belt, paired with black shoes carrying the "8" logo. Maki prefers shirts and pants for his civilian wear. Throughout the series, she goes through a remarkable metamorphosis from a reluctant Second Generation pyrokinetic burdened by family expectations to a confident leader among her squad, overcoming anxieties and forging powerful relationships with her comrades, reconciling with her family's legacy, and embodying strength and resolve in challenging situations.

Ability: Second Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Maki is a skilled fighter that Takehisa refers to as a witch. She excels at hand-to-hand combat and can easily disarm opponents like Arthur. Even the most powerful members of the Fire Force admire her adaptability. Maki, a former soldier in the Tokyo Military, has extensive expertise dealing with several opponents and easily overcoming greater rivals. She's also an accomplished instructor, teaching Shinra effective grappling tactics. Maki's small form conceals deceiving raw strength, allowing her to effortlessly move large metal pipes.

Shinra Kusakabe

Member(Main Character)

Shinra Kusakabe

Shinra Kusakabe, originally depicted as a slender youngster with short spiky black hair and crimson eyes, wears bunker gear bearing the number 8, indicating his brigade membership. Maki Oze altered his trousers to stop below the knees, preventing damage from his Ignition Ability. Off duty, he wears an orange coverall with patches reading "Company 8." In disguise, he has white hair, flame-style earrings, and tattoos resembling Adolla. He then reverts to black hair and takes off his earrings. Through soul resonance, he transforms into Shinrabanshō-man, with demonic looks and flame tattoos, similar to Hibachi Shinmon's doppelgänger. Throughout "Fire Force," Shinra grows from a confused rookie firefighter burdened by his "devil" reputation to a mature and responsible hero. He accepts the past and embraces his role in protecting society, forms deep bonds with his teammates, seeks truth about spontaneous human combustion, and strengthens his abilities through adversity.

Ability: Third and Fourth Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Shinra is one of the strongest characters in Fire Force, with great combat abilities. Despite being a novice, he faced veteran members and even defeated a Captain. His past as a Fire Soldier gives him the strength to save countless individuals from dangerous circumstances. Shinra effectively uses his Ignition Ability and hand-to-hand skills in combat, executing surprising break dancing-inspired maneuvers. He has incredible toughness, withstanding harsh beatings and extreme heat. He also has improved vision and an unbreakable resolve, which is motivated by his heroic goals and devotion to his family. Despite losing his pyrokinetic talents, he obtains immense physical and magical powers, establishing his place as one of the series' most powerful characters.

Arthur Boyle

Member

Arthur Boyle

Arthur Boyle, a thin young man with shaggy blonde hair and blue eyes, resembles Shinra, but with white pupils. He is dressed in bunker gear with blue lines and the number "8" patches, which represent his membership in the 8th Special Fire Brigade. Arthur, carrying a scabbard for his bladeless sword, endures tremendous physical problems after meeting Dragon, including losing his left forearm and receiving a scar over his right eye. Despite these difficulties, Arthur's character growth in "Fire Force" takes him from deluded knighthood dreams to accepting his duty as a mature Fire Soldier through self-discovery, team bonding, skill increase, inner conflict resolution, and newfound acceptance.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Arthur is among the strongest in the Special Fire Force, potentially the world. His immense strength, speed matching lightning, and exceptional durability make him formidable. In battle, he sliced through Dragon's armor effortlessly and split the world with his final attack, Violet Flash: Earth Divider. Arthur's stamina allows prolonged fights, showcasing his indomitable will to protect humanity as the Knight King.

Tamaki Kotatsu

Member

Tamaki Kotatsu

Tamaki Kotatsu in "Fire Force" undergoes a significant transformation from a timid rookie to a confident and skilled firefighter. Initially struggling with insecurity and her "Lucky Lecher Lure," she grows through training and missions, finding her purpose and earning respect from her team. Sporting a distinctive appearance, both on and off duty, she showcases resilience and strength as she faces challenges and bonds with her teammates, ultimately becoming a key player in the 8th Special Fire Brigade. Additionally, her Ignition Ability grants her cat-like characteristics, adding complexity to her character development.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Tamaki, first underestimated in Company 1, has latent qualities that she subsequently exploits, demonstrating rapid crisis management and adaptability. As a Third Generation pyrokinetic, her Ignition Ability protects her from burns and engulfs her in pink flames, giving her cat-like characteristics and increasing agility and movement. She can push herself with her tails, unleash powerful fire blasts, and evolve to coat her body in flames, increasing flexibility and allowing her to destroy big fire structures. She has improved speed and resilience, allowing her to dodge assaults and endure hits from powerful opponents..

Iris

Member (Sister)

Iris

Iris, a young lady with short blonde hair and blue eyes, often wears a habit with a veil, sandals, gloves, stockings, and a cross-shaped brooch. Her clothing features blue lines, indicating her participation in the Special Fire Force. In combat, she wears a neck protector and maybe a gas mask. Even her underwear has the same symbol. During cleaning ceremonies, she wears revealing light attire. In everyday life, Iris prefers classic but charming skirts and blouses. Throughout "Fire Force," Iris grows from a protected nun to a mature and independent person. She analyzes her views, faces hardships, and becomes a source of strength for her companions. Her journey includes reconciling her faith with the harsh facts of the world, demonstrating great emotional resilience and growth.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

After the finding of all eight Pillars, Iris developed increased pyrokinetic skills. She can generate little embers and project a protective aura of heat, sheltering herself and companions from flames, akin to Pan Ko Paat's Ignition Ability. This power appears to be linked to Iris' prayers and connection with the Adolla Realm.

Victor Licht

Member

Victor Licht

Victor Licht, with his unusual afro and drowsy, pupil-less eyes, begins as a mysterious scientist with ambiguous allegiance. Throughout the narrative, he reveals his previous involvement with Haijima Industries and becomes an important ally to the heroes, providing critical scientific expertise. When he joins Company 8, he dons bunker gear to symbolize his incorporation into the team. Victor's quest is one of atonement, as he seeks to atone for past wrongs and align himself with the greater good alongside his newfound companions.

Ability: Non-Powered Human

Licht lacks Ignition Ability but makes up for it with extraordinary intelligence and knowledge of chemistry. He made explosives from Infernal Remains and can assess skills he can't fully comprehend. Licht's strategic preparation and foresight, demonstrated by providing an escape route for Joker, are critical to the Fire Force's missions against Infernals and big fires.

Vulcan Joseph

Member

Vulcan Joseph

Vulcan Joseph, a skilled engineer in "Fire Force," undergoes profound character development. Initially distrusting of others due to his family's history, he eventually confronts ethical dilemmas and explores his legacy. Through his journey, he discovers his true strengths and contributions to Company 8, evolving into a more confident and principled individual, all while sporting his signature attire: a ram mask, goggles, and animal-themed shirts over his bunker gear.

Ability: Non-Powered Human

Vulcan's primary expertise is engineering, as seen by his ability to build and maintain Company 8's equipment, including specialized gear such as Maki's Iron Owls. Despite his profession, he is physically fit thanks to intensive training and can participate in missions including both logistical support and warfare.

Lisa Isaribi

Member

Lisa Isaribi

Lisa Isaribi appears in "Fire Force" as a shy girl who is forced into joining the wicked White Clad. She evolves as a result of her interactions with protagonist Shinra and the Fire Force, which cause her to question her loyalties before eventually joining them. Her narrative focuses on repentance, progress, and the power of choice. Lisa is shown as a voluptuous young woman with light red hair twisted into a knot ponytail, burnt orange eyes, and striking tattoos. She starts off in a white crop top and dark slacks, then switches to the Ashen Flame's outfit, which includes a white robe and armored boots, and finally to Company 8's orange jumpsuit with a plunging black crop top.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Lisa, a Third Generation, uses her Ignition Ability to create octopus-like tendrils. She attacks opponents with blunt force or grapples them, frequently appearing as a massive octopus. Her flames have magnetic qualities, which allow them to lock onto magnetic fields created by live people and machines. Opponents can exploit these features by nullifying them.
