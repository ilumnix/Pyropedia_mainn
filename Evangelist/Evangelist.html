White-Clad

Evangelist

Leader

Evangelist

The Evangelist is a mysterious, hooded creature with a diabolical look, including vacant eye sockets and star-like sparks within. She wears a light-colored robe with two horns sprouting from her hood. She holds enormous strength and is related with the Sun God's emblem.She persuaded Sumire Sugita of the importance of the Great Cataclysm and oversaw its unsuccessful effort. She uses religion to deceive survivors and formed the Holy Sol Temple with her disciple Yona, who imitates Raffles Smith. She organizes the Second Cataclysm and gathers disciples such as Faerie and Dragon for the promised day. The Evangelist is knowledgeable about spontaneous human combustion, Adolla Burst, and the Adolla world. She kidnaps children for research, which results in the acquisition of Haumea as the Second Pillar. She appears in pivotal moments, causing Mari Kusakabe to be blinded and influencing Shō Kusakabe's destiny.

Ability:

The Evangelist has remarkable skills, including going through the Tear in Space, generating earthquakes, and inducing severe pain in people who gaze straight at her. She can provide pyrokinetic skills known as Fourth Generation to individuals with the Adolla Link, known as Grace, which she may forfeit at anytime.

Shō Kusakabe

Commander

Shō Kusakabe

Shō, a young teenager with blazing red eyes and pristine white hair, resembles an angel. He wears a White-Clad outfit and wields a katana. After quitting the White-Clad, he dresses in showy civilian attire. Shō, who was initially emotionally distant and apathetic to his family, now commands respect as Commander of the Knights of the Ashen Flame. He is cool and ready to kill without hesitation. However, reconnecting with Shinra and his lost memories causes emotional maturation, including tears and curiosity about the outside world. Shō's Adolla Burst ignited mysteriously, resulting in his kidnapping by Haumea and believing in the Evangelist as his parent.

Ability: Fourth Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Shō's Fourth Generation pyrokinetic ability allows him to manipulate time and draw heat from the expanding universe. He no longer needs Grace during the Cataclysm because of the enhanced Adolla bond.Shō is a skilled swordsman who wields his katana with accuracy and uses the flat side to incapacitate opponents without killing them. Despite his brother's pyrokinetic abilities, Shō easily defeats him in combat.His incredible speed lets him to stay up with fast opponents like Shinra and Joker, even when his time manipulation powers are negated. Shō's extraordinary strength allows him to easily launch Shinra across rooms and destroy solid structures.

Haumea

Member

Haumea

Haumea is a young woman with long pale-blonde hair put in a bun and a golden crown-like helmet that hides her facial characteristics. She's wearing a loose white onesie with dark cloth underneath and white footwear. Her gown bears the image of a red sun. Initially quiet, she develops a humorous and wicked attitude, motivated by hearing humanity's deepest thoughts. Despite her brutality, she has sympathy for Charon, who has shown her unconditional love. Haumea has been with the Evangelist for most of her life and was charged with abducting Shō Kusakabe, the Third Pillar, at the age of five. Her Adolla Burst activation caused mental strain, which triggered her cruel tendencies as a coping mechanism.

Ability: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Haumea, a Third Generation Pyrokinetic, uses Electric Shock to manipulate electrical signals in order to control nerves and behaviors, communicate remotely, and read thoughts. She has the ability to control Demon Infernals and view occurrences that ordinary people cannot see. Haumea obtained extra powers as a result of other Pillars' sacrifices, such as immunity to harm during stopped time, precognition, and the capacity to launch a deadly light attack. However, her skills are ineffectual against plasma-based abilities like Arthur's.

Charon

Member

Charon

Charon is a tall, muscular man with light-colored hair who is frequently seen wearing a blindfold with a red cross. His outfit resembles a martial arts costume, with dark gloves, a belt displaying the same cross insignia, and a toeless Tabi. He is committed to the Evangelist and has been Haumea's guardian since she was a youngster. Despite his annoyance and irritation with Haumea's aggressive manner, Charon is crafty and confident in fight, frequently simulating Third Generation Ignition Ability. His absolute love for Haumea motivates him to support the White-Clad's cause, thinking that the Evangelist would put an end to her suffering. Twelve years ago, he assisted Haumea in abducting the Third Pillar, Shō Kusakabe, demonstrating his commitment to the goal.

Ability: Second Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Charon excelled at hand-to-hand combat, delivering strong attacks and grabs thanks to his great strength and resilient build. His body could resist severe injury and heal swiftly. Charon's Second Generation talent allowed him to absorb kinetic energy from assaults and turn it into thermal energy, resulting in massive explosions. With minor gestures, he could redirect this energy and produce many forms of explosions. However, he was unable to absorb and release energy at the same time, making him vulnerable to attacks. There was a limit to how much energy he could absorb; deflecting a nuclear explosion from Nataku drained him, while receiving repeated blasts from Hinawa killed him. Despite this, Charon shown amazing competence against formidable pyrokinetic fighters like as Kurono and Shinmon, earning the latter's respect as a "monster."

Arrow

Member

Arrow

Arrow, a loyal member of the White-Clad, has long dark-blue hair, a white outfit with a burqa-like mask, and characteristic arrow-shaped irises. Despite her calm and solemn appearance, she is committed to the Evangelist's goals and believes in a future global change, regardless of human feelings. Arrow is committed to serving Sho, demonstrating unflinching dedication and a sense of responsibility. After leaving the White Clad with Sho, she becomes more expressive, understanding, and hopeful, collaborating well with Company 8 amid a disaster.

Ability: Second Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Arrow, a Third Generation, with a powerful Ignition Ability that allows her to manufacture flaming bows and arrows with her left arm. Her excellent vision allows her to launch precise and devastating assaults over long distances. She specializes in stealth, using her powerful arrows to sever limbs and pierce past barriers. Arrow's abilities are compared to those of a tank, and she has earned the trust of allies due to her overwhelming power and adaptability in battle.

Dr. Giovanni

Member

Dr. Giovanni

Dr. Giovanni, who resembles a disease doctor, begins as a cold antagonist in "Fire Force," sending Evangelist agents to infiltrate Company 3. His look changes from black attire and a white cane to a skinnier Special Fire Force jacket with mechanics concealed beneath a big cloak. His past as a disillusioned scientist gradually develops, giving his character dimension. Interactions with allies and foes reveal his personal problems. Giovanni eventually goes through a redemption arc, questioning his commitment to the White-Clad and trying for redemption, demonstrating the series' deep character development.
