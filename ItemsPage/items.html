List Of Items

Excalibur

Excalibur is a sword with a distinct design, including a white hilt with spiraling tape and a cross. When activated, it sends out a blue aura from the top, forming a plasma blade. The white sheath enhances its appearance. Arthur also carries miniature copies of Excalibur's hilt, which work as knives but have little heat resistance and melt after a single usage.

Bug

The White-Clad uses bugs, which are attracted to flames like the Adolla Burst, to locate the Eight Pillars. Rekka Hoshimiya uses glass vials to release bugs by pressing them on targets. He discovered Adolla Burst in Shinra Kusakabe in this manner. The bugs may alter people, as shown with Nataku Son and Haran, who transformed into a Demon Infernal during a battle. To avoid interrogation, another member disguises himself as an Infernal using a flaw.

Seven-Style Fire Fighting Battleaxe

The Seven-Style Fire Fighting Battleaxe, designed by Haijima Industries for the Special Fire Brigades, discharges sacred spikes when the trigger is released from the firing hammer. It features a safety switch.

Iron Owls

Vulcan Invented the Iron Owls to enhance Maki's power. Maki incorporates Flare and Sputter into her twin weapons, allowing them to fly and be commanded for devastating attacks. She is skilled in hand-to-hand combat and uses the Owl Eyes technique, which involves attaching cameras to the Iron Owls and viewing footage through a headset and a monitor on Company 8's Special Armored Fire Engine. It makes an excellent spectating apparatus and also Vulcan Invented this tools.

Core Annihilating Pile Bunker

Haijima Industries created the Core Annihilating Pile Bunker for the Fire Force, which includes a piston, spiked shaft, and recoil springs. Worn on the user's hand, it easily impales an Infernal's core.

Powder Medicine

The Powder Medicine Is a powdered substance whose main component is the ashes of Infernals.[1] It can induce explosions, powerful enough to cause a building's ceiling to collapse, and was given to Joker by Victor Licht The substance is contained in a bottle, which appears to keep it inactive.

Special Armoured Fire Engines

The Fire Engine is a big vehicle that can accommodate at least four brigade members. It has fire extinguishers, hoses, and a speakerphone. The front features the Special Fire Brigade crest, which includes the brigade's number, such as "08" for the 8th Special Fire Brigade.

Special fire Extinguising Bullet

Takehisa fires a foam-filled pressurized bullet to blanket and cool the target, serving as a fire extinguisher alternative to immobilize an Infernal.

Special Shield

Members of the Fire Force utilize the Special Shield as a protective tool to defend themselves from flames and attacks.

Whip-Sword

The Whip-Sword is an extensible sword that can generate air currents.
